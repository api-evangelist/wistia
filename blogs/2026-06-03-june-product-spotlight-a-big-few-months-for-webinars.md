Wistia announced major updates to its webinar platform, including embeddable webinars on custom domains, real-time playback capabilities, and expanded marketing automation integrations. The company also released new AI-powered editing tools like Remix for repurposing content, improved team collaboration features, and enhanced analytics tracking with expanded UTM parameters and AI-generated event recaps.
